FCHIP

FairBanks Community Health Intelligence Platform

Your health, our mission.

FCHIP turns community and facility data — plus maps and climate signals — into earlier alerts so FairBanks Community Reach and our Medical Centre can act before problems grow.

FairBanks Reach Partnership

How FCHIP works

  1. Capture — CHW mobile forms, secure EMR/HMS APIs, climate feeds
  2. Analyse — AI risk scores, GIS clustering, climate fusion
  3. Alert — CHW worklists, facility dashboard, partner views
  4. Act — outreach, referrals, stock planning, follow-up

Platform architecture

What FCHIP helps predict

  • Disease surveillance (climate-aware)
  • Maternal health risk
  • NCD hotspots
  • Child health and immunisation gaps
  • Medicine demand
  • Community Reach cascade metrics

Example: climate-aware early warning

Fever reports + heavy rainfall + GIS clustering → FCHIP alert → outreach, testing, and pharmacy pre-stocking before caseloads spike.
