# FCHIP — feature cards (CMS-ready)

Use as six cards under “Deep technology” or as a grid under “What FCHIP does”.

---

### Card 1 — AI & early warning
**Title:** AI-assisted risk scoring  
**Body:** Spot rising disease risk, maternal complications, and NCD hotspots earlier — with explainable alerts for field and facility teams.  
**Icon cue:** pulse / alert

### Card 2 — GIS mapping
**Title:** Maps that show where risk sits  
**Body:** Visualise cases, climate overlays, and risk zones by village, parish, or facility catchment.  
**Icon cue:** map pin

### Card 3 — Climate fusion
**Title:** Climate meets community health  
**Body:** Rainfall, heat, and extreme-weather feeds join CHW and clinic signals for climate-aware early detection.  
**Icon cue:** cloud / rain

### Card 4 — Mobile capture
**Title:** Built for CHWs and VHTs  
**Body:** Offline-capable forms for household visits, vitals, referrals, and follow-up — then sync when connectivity returns.  
**Icon cue:** smartphone

### Card 5 — Facility dashboards
**Title:** One view for the Medical Centre  
**Body:** Trends, alerts, completed referrals, and outreach priorities for clinicians and programme managers.  
**Icon cue:** dashboard

### Card 6 — Secure EMR / HMS APIs
**Title:** Connect systems you already use  
**Body:** Authenticated, consent-aware data APIs let existing EMR and hospital systems share clinical data into FCHIP — without ripping out current software.  
**Icon cue:** link / shield

## Short strip (Home or Reach)

**Line:** FCHIP turns community and facility data into earlier action.  
**Pills:** Capture · Analyse · Alert · Act
