## Intro (above the fold)

FCHIP is FairBanks’ community health intelligence platform. It sits on our Community Reach model and turns information from the field and our facilities into clearer early warnings — so care can move from reacting after illness to preventing problems sooner.

FairBanks is more than a medical centre. We connect community members, community health workers (CHWs / VHTs), outreach programmes, our Medical Centre, research partners, and livelihoods support — including CHIS (Community Health Insurance Scheme). FCHIP is the technology layer that helps that whole loop learn and improve.

---

## Why FCHIP matters

In many communities, clinics only see people after they are already sick. Outreach notes stay in paper books or separate systems. Weather and place-based risk rarely join the picture. FCHIP helps FairBanks bring these signals together so teams can spot rising risk earlier — for example fever clusters after heavy rain, mothers who need closer follow-up, or neighbourhoods where blood pressure and diabetes need more screening.

---

## How FCHIP works

1. **Capture** — Offline mobile forms for CHWs/VHTs, secure links to facility systems (EMR/HMS), and climate feeds.  
2. **Analyse** — AI-assisted risk scores, GIS clustering, and climate–health fusion.  
3. **Alert** — Worklists for CHWs, facility dashboards, and partner / district views.  
4. **Act** — Outreach, referrals, stock planning, and follow-up — then learn and improve again.

## What FCHIP connects

FCHIP is built to receive structured health information from FairBanks and partner sources along our Community Reach cascade, including:

- Community Health Workers and Village Health Teams (home visits, referrals, follow-up)
- Schools and community outreach programmes
- FairBanks Medical Centre and partner clinics and hospitals
- Existing facility record systems, through secure data connections (so clinics do not have to replace software they already use)
- Pharmacies and medicine points, where data is available
- Maternal and child health, chronic disease, and geriatric (Gericare) programmes
- Climate and weather feeds, combined with maps (GIS), for climate-aware early detection

## Deep technology, rooted in FairBanks

FCHIP combines practical tools with deeper intelligence:

- Artificial intelligence and machine learning for risk scoring and early warning  
- GIS mapping of disease and risk by place  
- Climate API integration (rainfall, heat, and related signals) fused with health data  
- Mobile, offline-capable data capture for CHWs/VHTs  
- Secure cloud sync and facility dashboards  
- Safe APIs so existing EMR / hospital systems can share clinical data into FCHIP in real time  

This is the Data & Feedback layer of FairBanks Community Reach — learn, improve, and serve again.

## What FCHIP helps predict

*(Insert `fchip_use_cases.png` here.)*

**Disease surveillance** — Rising fever reports in neighbouring villages, plus heavy rainfall and a tight map cluster, can flag possible outbreak risk so outreach and testing can move early.

**Maternal health** — Home-visit signals can highlight higher-risk pregnancies and missed antenatal care so CHWs and clinicians can follow up sooner.

**Chronic disease (NCDs)** — Blood pressure and screening data from camps, clinics, and workplaces can show where hypertension and diabetes need targeted action.

**Child health** — Growth, immunisation, and diarrhoea patterns from homes, schools, and clinics can trigger nutrition and immunisation support.

**Medicine demand** — Disease trends, climate patterns, and use at the facility can help plan stock before shortages.

**Cascade metrics** — CHW activity, completed referrals, school sessions, and CHIS / livelihood signals help managers close gaps in the Community Reach loop.
