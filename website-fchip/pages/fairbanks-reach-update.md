## Suggested short intro tweak (if the page opens with clinic-only wording)

FairBanks Community Reach is how we care beyond the clinic walls. Communities identify needs. CHWs and VHTs bridge homes and care. Our programmes bring screening, education, maternal and child health, and home visits. FairBanks Medical Centre anchors clinical care. Research and partners help us learn. Livelihoods and CHIS help families stay resilient. Through **FCHIP**, we strengthen the Data & Feedback loop so we can learn, improve, and serve again.

## New section — FCHIP (Data & Feedback)

### Heading
FCHIP — our community health intelligence layer

### Body

FCHIP (FairBanks Community Health Intelligence Platform) is a FairBanks component that sits on Community Reach. It helps turn community and facility information — together with maps and climate signals — into earlier alerts and clearer programme insight.

It does not replace CHWs, outreach, or the Medical Centre. It helps them work with better timing and better visibility.
